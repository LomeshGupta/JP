Meet Our Founder

Mr. S.K. Gupta

Director



Mr. S.K. Gupta, the esteemed Director of J.P. International School, is a visionary leader in the field of education. With over two decades of dedicated service, he has passionately steered the institution towards excellence. Mr. Gupta's unwavering commitment to providing holistic education has transformed the school into a nurturing haven for young minds. His innovative approach, coupled with a deep understanding of pedagogy, has created an environment where students flourish academically and develop as well-rounded individuals. Under his guidance, the school has become a beacon of knowledge, fostering a love for learning and instilling values that shape future leaders.
